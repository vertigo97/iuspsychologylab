VIRTUAL REALITY LAB

About the VR Lab

Recently, with the technological progress provided by virtual reality, the course of scientific research has taken on a whole new perspective, and as a response to these new trends, our VR Lab was created by Dr Pinar Unal-Aydin.

The Virtual Reality-Lab of the Psychology Department of the International University of Sarajevo will offer our students and staff a whole new perspective on research regarding mental disorders, and will be, for now, used for research purposes, covering areas of test anxiety and phobias.

Anxiety disorders, including test anxiety and phobias, can be highly assessed, tested and treated via Virtual Reality, which will become a great benefit for our future research plans and opportunities.
